# Scheduling & Confirmation (Property Tree)

**Version:** V2.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-31

---

## Purpose / Outcome
Schedule inspections and obtain tenant confirmation properly, with correct sync to Inspection Express.

## Trigger
Quarterly routine inspection due OR ingoing/outgoing inspection required.

## Steps
1) Schedule inspection in Property Tree and confirm it syncs to Inspection Express  
2) Send ~10 day email with proposed time window  
3) Send ~9 day SMS requesting confirmation  
4) Follow up non-responders  
5) Once confirmed, update inspection status to **Confirmed** in Property Tree (manual)  
6) Send 24-hour reminder

## Quality checks
- Appears in Inspection Express (sync confirmed)
- Tenant confirmation recorded
- Property Tree set to Confirmed
