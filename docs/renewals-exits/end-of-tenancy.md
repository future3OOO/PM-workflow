# End of Tenancy & Lease Break

**Version:** V2.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-31

---

## Goal
End tenancies cleanly with clear instructions, outgoing inspection, bond processing, and full records. For lease breaks, follow your published “fixed-term lease break guide”.

## Triggers
- tenant ending at expiry / periodic notice
- mutual agreement to end early
- lease break request received in writing

## Outputs (definition of done)
- vacate pack sent and filed
- outgoing inspection completed and filed
- bond process completed (or dispute route started)
- property relet or handed back to owner
- lease break fee and ongoing rent obligations managed and documented (where applicable)

---

## Stage 1 — Confirm end details in writing
- confirm end date and key return process
- confirm outgoing inspection timing

---

## Stage 2 — Vacate pack
Send vacate pack with:
- cleaning expectations
- rubbish removal
- keys/remotes return
- final inspection process
- bond process outline

Template: [Notice & Email Templates](../day-to-day/notice-email-templates.md)

---

## Stage 3 — Outgoing inspection
- schedule in Property Tree; sync to Inspection Express
- compare to ingoing report
- document tenant damage vs fair wear/tear
- publish and file report

---

## Stage 4 — Bond processing
- lodge bond refund/transfer form or Bond Hub process as used
- file outcomes into Property Tree

---

## Stage 5 — Lease break (fixed-term)
Use the published guide and internal workflow:

!!! warning "Lease Break — Key Rules"
    - Request must be in writing
    - Landlord approval required before proceeding
    - Vacate date becomes a commitment once relisted
    - Fee typically one week rent (tenant must check their agreement), payable on approval
    - Rent continues until replacement tenancy starts or fixed end date reached (whichever first)
    - Property relisted immediately; viewings ASAP; tenant should maximise access
    - Require 2–3 clear days vacant possession before new tenancy begins

---

## Handover
- if re-letting: start [Leasing Lifecycle](../leasing/leasing-lifecycle.md)
- if handing back: confirm keys, final statement, close property file with notes
