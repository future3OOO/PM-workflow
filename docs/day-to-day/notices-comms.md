# Notices & Communications

**Version:** V2.0  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-24

---

## Goal
Use the correct notice and service method, preserve evidence, and keep communications calm and traceable.

## Scope
- rent increases
- access/inspection communications
- breaches / remedy pathway (including escalation)
- end of tenancy communications
- lease break communications and agreements

---

## Operating rules
1) Verify service addresses and service method before issuing formal notices.  
2) Keep a copy of the notice and delivery evidence in Property Tree docs.  
3) Use official Tenancy Services forms/templates where required.  
4) Keep language factual and calm. Document the next step and deadline.  
5) If uncertain, escalate internally before sending a notice (risk control).

---

## Escalation ladder (tenant issues)
1) Friendly request (written) + deadline  
2) Follow-up reminder with evidence request (photos)  
3) If repeat or severe: formal notice pathway (verify legality first)  
4) Follow-up inspection if needed for evidence  
5) Tribunal/next steps only when required (document thoroughly)
