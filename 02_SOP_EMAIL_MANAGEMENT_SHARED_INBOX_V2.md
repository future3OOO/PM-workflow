# SOP — Email Management (Owner inbox + hello@ shared inbox) (Version 2)

**Version:** V2 (Full)  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-24

---

## Roles
- VA: triage, follow-ups, closing loops, filing into Property Tree.
- Owner/PM: approvals, decisions, escalations.

## Labels (recommended)
Property label:
- PROP – <StreetName Short>

Status labels:
- URGENT – 24h
- ACTION – Today
- APPROVAL – Owner
- WAITING – Reply
- CLOSED – Filed

## Daily workflow (VA)
1) Clear URGENT first  
2) Clear ACTION  
3) Summarise approval emails to owner/PM (short)  
4) Chase WAITING items  
5) Close and file threads

## Filing rule
Any decision affecting tenancy dates, money, compliance, inspections, or maintenance must be filed to Property Tree docs (PDF if needed) under 06 Notices & Key Correspondence.
